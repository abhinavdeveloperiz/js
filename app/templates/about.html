Our Story

We are a Dubai-based trading company specializing in premium-quality cashew kernels and a wide range of nuts sourced from leading global origins, including Africa, the USA, Vietnam, India, China, and other major producing regions. Our product portfolio includes cashews, almonds, walnuts, pistachios, and other carefully selected nuts, sourced to meet international quality standards. With more than 20 years of experience in the global nut and kernel trade, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, consistency, and trust. We work closely with reputed international suppliers and trusted global companies, ensuring that only the finest-quality products reach our customers. Our focus is on delivering premium-grade products, maintaining long-term partnerships, and ensuring customer satisfaction through transparent and efficient business practices. Over the years, we have established a strong global presence and continue to expand our network across key international markets. Based in Dubai, we leverage the city’s strategic position as a global trade hub to efficiently connect suppliers and buyers worldwide. Our commitment is to quality, integrity, and sustainable long-term growth in the international nut trade industry.
